# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent unless there is strong evidence otherwise.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people.
- Make room for contributors with different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on identity or background
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith participation
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and discussions that violate these expectations.
